Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

When you use Maya on Telegram, we collect:

  • Telegram user ID and username — to identify your account.
  • Messages you send to Maya — to generate responses and build your memory profile.
  • Key facts — details Maya extracts from conversations (your name, preferences, etc.). You can view these with /memory and delete them with /forget.
  • Payment information — processed by Stripe. We never see or store your credit card number.

How we use your data

  • To provide Maya's conversational AI service.
  • To remember things about you and personalize responses.
  • To process payments via Stripe.
  • To improve the service (aggregate, anonymized usage statistics only).

What we don't do

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use your messages to train AI models.
  • We do not share your conversations or memories with anyone.
  • We do not show you ads.

Your control

  • Use /memory to see everything Maya knows about you.
  • Use /forget [fact] to delete any memory.
  • Use /export to download your conversation history (Plus).
  • To delete your account entirely, message us and we will remove all your data.

Data storage

Your data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway. Connections are encrypted in transit. We use Stripe for payment processing — see Stripe's privacy policy.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Message Maya and ask, or contact us at privacy@getmaya.ai.
